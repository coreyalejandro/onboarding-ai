Examples: Natural Language vs ML‑Friendly
Natural Language
"Please analyze this customer chat transcript and tell me what's going on and what we should do next."
ML‑Friendly (Anthropic Framework)
Role & Objective: Analyze the transcript and recommend the next action. Constraints & Safety: No personal data exposure; do not invent facts. Process: Identify intent → detect sentiment → map to playbook. Output Format: JSON object { intent, sentiment, recommended_action }. Success Criteria: Intent is one of ["billing","tech_support","cancellation"]; action references playbook ID.
Structured prompts enable consistent evaluation and routing.
